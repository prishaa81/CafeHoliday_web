Our Story

Mira Road's most beloved hangout

Nestled in Gaurav Garden, Cafe Holiday is where Mira Bhayandar comes alive. Our kitchen travels the world — Pan Asian dim sums, fresh sushi, butter chicken, crispy continental bites — all under one beautifully lit roof.

Whether it's a quiet date night, a rowdy night out with friends, or a celebration soundtracked by live music, we deliver the full experience every time. Beautiful, cozy, and aesthetic — just like our guests say.
